Assetc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/def/openadr#Asset

A type of Resource that represents a specific collection of physical loads. Resources can be composed of Assets, and an Asset may be Resource, but Assets cannot be further decomposed into multiple Assets or Resources.
Is defined by
https://w3id.org/def/openadr
